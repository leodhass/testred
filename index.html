About Redstone Automotives

Get to know about our automotive expertise

Redstone was established in 2018 in Namakkal with the primary objective of meeting the demand for NTC tires. Acknowledging the shifting market requirements for retread tires, we relocated to Chennai in 2019. Following the closure of the MAN INDIA plant, we acquired the entire inventory of spare parts. Presently, the distribution of spare parts has become our central business focus.Now our services extend across the Asia Pacific, SAARC, and Central Middle East regions, ensuring reliable support and coverage throughout these key markets. .

We pride ourselves on transparent pricing, quality workmanship, and exceptional customer service. Every vehicle that enters our site receives the same level of care and attention we would give our own.
